Media In-Charge

As the Media In-Charge of the DEKIMU SPACE Program, we are dedicated to shaping and amplifying the voice of our initiative. Our responsibilities include:

  • Managing communication strategies to effectively convey our vision and mission.
  • Promoting DEKIMU across both digital and traditional platforms.
  • Ensuring our message reaches and resonates with diverse audiences.
  • Showcasing the transformative work of our core pillars: Public Speaking, Leadership Excellence, Academic Excellence, and Mental Health & Wellness.
  • Creating content that fosters a positive and consistent image for DEKIMU.
  • Highlighting the impact and stories of our members and activities.

Our goal is to ensure that every aspect of our communication reflects the values and aspirations of the DEKIMU SPACE Program.
